## Enterprise Cybersecurity Risk Register

This deliverable documents the cybersecurity risks identified during the TechEase Solutions Threat Landscape Risk Assessment.

The risk register follows a structured enterprise risk management approach by identifying cybersecurity risks, evaluating their potential business impact, determining the likelihood of occurrence, calculating overall risk scores, and recommending mitigation strategies.

The workbook contains three worksheets:

### Worksheet 1 – Enterprise Risk Register
A comprehensive inventory of identified cybersecurity risks, including impact, likelihood, overall risk score, business owner, mitigation recommendations, and remediation status.

### Worksheet 2 – Cybersecurity Risk Heat Map
A visual representation of organizational risk exposure using a traditional 5×5 likelihood versus business impact matrix. The heat map highlights the highest-priority cybersecurity risks requiring executive attention.

### Worksheet 3 – Risk Scoring Methodology
Defines the standardized methodology used to assess and prioritize cybersecurity risks, including impact definitions, likelihood ratings, scoring calculations, risk categories, and alignment with recognized cybersecurity frameworks.
